Biography

The Story

Eazy OGee is one of Johannesburg's most authentic voices in Hip-Hop. With a sound rooted in the streets of Joburg and lyrics that reflect real lived experience, Eazy has built a reputation as an artist who doesn't compromise.

Known for his raw delivery and cinematic storytelling, Eazy draws inspiration from the hardships, victories, and culture of life in South Africa. His music carries weight — every bar intentional, every hook unforgettable.

Signed to SterkOpad Entertainment, Eazy OGee continues to push boundaries and build a legacy that will last beyond the music.
